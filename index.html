About Me


I am a Consultant Trauma and Orthopaedic Surgeion based in Bury St Edmunds. Im am specalised in hip surgery, and providing care to patients with hip injuries and disorders, on top of providing care to patients that have problems post hip replacement.

In August 2019 I was appointed Hip Specialist Surgeon at West Suffolk Hospital, Bury St Edmunds. I was later appointed as the lead for Hip Fracture care in West Suffolk Hospital, and am always working to improve the care and service we provide. I provide appointments for all NHS patients at St Edmunds Hosputal, as well as those with private health insureance, or patients who wish to self-fund their treatment.

With a Postgraduate Diploma in Medical Educastion from Cardiff University, I am involved in clinical research reltaed to the hip, having multiple publications, national and international presentations, on top of on-going projects.
