About Me


I am a Consultant Trauma and Orthopaedic Surgeon based in Bury St Edmunds. I am specialised in hip surgery, and providing care to patients with hip injuries and disorders, as well as providing care to patients that have problems post hip replacement.


In August 2019 I was appointed Hip Specialist Surgeon at West Suffolk Hospital, Bury St Edmunds. I was later appointed as the lead for Hip Fracture Care in West Suffolk Hospital, and am always working to improve the care and service we provide. I provide appointments for all NHS patients at St Edmunds Hospital, as well as those with private health insurance, or patients who wish to self-fund their treatment.


With a Postgraduate Diploma in Medical Education from Cardiff University, I am involved in clinical research related to the hip, having multiple publications, national and international presentations, as well as ongoing projects.
